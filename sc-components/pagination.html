Pagination

Pagination lists can be used for supplementary navigation

Simple

Use <ol class="c-pagination">

  2. 1
  4. 14
  5. 15
  6. 16
  8. 32

Round

Use <ol class="c-pagination--round">

  2. 1
  4. 14
  5. 15
  6. 16
  8. 32

Square

Use <ol class="c-pagination--square">

  2. 1
  4. 14
  5. 15
  6. 16
  8. 32
